AI for Healthcare

The Mayo Clinic Artificial Intelligence Symposium will bring together researchers from Mayo Clinic and external experts to learn about current AI activities in the healthcare domain and the rich varied possibilities of AI for healthcare.

Register

For the Care of Tomorrow

Healthcare is an area with great opportunity for Artificial Intelligence. The vast volumes of machine-processable data generated in healthcare both enable and require the application of modern advanced AI methodologies.

Register

AI + Clinical Data

The large number of news stories and reports of AI in the healthcare domain has created an environment filled with inflated expectations, baseless fears, and mismatches between use cases and technologies.

Register

Previous Next

Mayo Clinic, driven by its values, adopts a future-forward approach to leading health care transformation. Leveraging medical excellence and digital health sciences, artificial intelligence plays a critical role in supporting the Mayo 2030 strategy in achieving the mission of curing and preventing diseases, connecting people everywhere, and transforming healthcare through platforms. The Mayo Clinic Artificial Intelligence Symposium aims to bring the health care AI community together to learn about current activities, share best practices, and foster collaborations toward digital health and medicine.
